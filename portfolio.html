Investment Game

This project is a simulation-based game coded in Python where players make investment choices to build long-term wealth. The game introduces concepts of risk, return, and diversification by allowing players to allocate resources across different asset types such as stocks, bonds, and alternative investments. Each turn reflects market dynamics, with outcomes influenced by both probability and strategic decision-making. The goal is to grow wealth steadily while managing risk
